mission "Cargo Smuggling [0]"
	name "走私到<planet>。"
	job
	repeat
	description "偷运<cargo>到<destination>而不被行星执法部门扫描或抓住。付款方式为<payment>。"
	cargo "Illegal Cargo" 5 2 .1
	illegal 40000 `除了罚款外，你的非法货物也被没收。海盗们听到这个消息会很不高兴。`
	stealth
	to offer
		or
			"reputation: Pirate" > -100
			"combat rating" > 150
	source
		government "Pirate"
	destination
		distance 3 6
		government "Republic" "Free Worlds" "Syndicate" "Neutral" "Pirate"
	on visit
		dialog phrase "generic cargo on visit"
	on abort
		"reputation: Pirate" -= 1
		dialog phrase "generic aborted pirate cargo mission"
	on fail
		"reputation: Pirate" -= 1
	on complete
		payment 4000 300
		dialog phrase "generic completed pirate cargo mission"
